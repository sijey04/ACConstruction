# AC Construction Co. & Services

A corporate website and lead-management admin portal for **AC Construction Co. & Services**, a Marshall Islands-based construction and infrastructure company under Kabkondrikdrik Enterprises Inc. (KEI). 

This project serves as the digital storefront for the company's public and government contracting services, while providing a secure backend dashboard for the team to manage client inquiries and project leads.

## 🏢 About the Company
AC Construction Co. & Services is focused on construction, infrastructure support, land reclamation, earthmoving, and development projects in the Marshall Islands. Established by the Bing family, the company is committed to creating local jobs and building the future of the nation.

## ✨ Features

### Public-Facing Website (Frontend)
*   **Home:** High-impact landing page highlighting core services and company mission.
*   **About Us:** Company background, connection to KEI, and the overarching mission/vision.
*   **Services Portfolio:** Categorized breakdown of offerings (Construction, Earthworks & Marine, Project Contracting, Materials & Manufacturing).
*   **Contact & Lead Generation:** A user-friendly inquiry form that feeds directly into the admin dashboard.

### Admin Dashboard (Backend)
*   **Secure Authentication:** Protected login for AC Construction staff.
*   **Inquiry Management:** A data table to view, sort, and manage incoming messages from the public site.
*   **Lead Tracking:** Update the status of inquiries (e.g., New, Contacted, Closed).
